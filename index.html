Instructions:

Game Mode : Search and Destroy!

Goal: Kill all the enemy troops while loosing as few of your own as possible!

How to Play:

  • Red plays first and keeps his chips on cells in the first two columns. To keep a chip
    click once on a red chip, then click on a empty cell in the first two columns.
  • Blue then does the same and keeps his chips on cells in the last two columns.
  • Now Red and Blue take turns and move 1 chip in each turn. A chip can only be moved to a neighboring node.
  • When two chips of different colors land on the same node, the one with lesser strength gets annhilated.
    If both are the same strength, both get annhilated.
